## **Payment Collection Events**

| **Event Name**           | **Event Type**           | **Description**                                                                                |
|--------------------------|--------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------               |
| **Payment Successful**   | `payment_successful`    | Triggered when a payment is successfully processed.                                             |
| **Payment Failed**       | `payment_failed`        | Occurs when a payment attempt fails due to customer errors, declined cards, or other issues.    |
