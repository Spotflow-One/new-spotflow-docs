## Quick Start: Launch in Minutes

Want to get started off using Spotflow right away? Follow these essential steps to go from zero to payment processing:

### Follow these steps

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1 : Login to your account or Sign up">
    Log in using your email and password, or sign in with Google. If you don't have an account yet, you can create one at [https://app.spotflow.co/signup](https://app.spotflow.co/signup).

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Step 2: Complete KYB Verification">
    **How It Works**

   This critical, compliance-driven step confirms your identity and the legitimacy of your business with legal and regulatory standards. You must complete this to unlock full platform access, including processing and payouts.

    <Card title="Business Verification" horizontal icon="box" href="https://app.spotflow.co/signup">Submit business registration documents for manual review and compliance clearance.</Card>

    <Card title="For More Details" horizontal icon="repeat" href="/expert-desk/verification-process">Visit the KYB verification process page</Card>

  </Accordion>
