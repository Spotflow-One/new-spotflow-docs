## Introduction

This guide compares the MoR model with the traditional Payment Gateway approach, helping you understand the advantages Spotflow brings to your business.

## What It Means For You

Choosing Spotflow as your MoR means we become the legal seller to your customers, allowing you to:

- Launch Fast in Africa: Instantly access local payment methods crucial for conversion (Mobile Money, Bank Transfers) without integrating multiple PSPs.
- Decentralize Risk: Spotflow assumes liability for compliance, local laws, and currency conversion.
- Focus on Product: Skip setting up local entities, avoid handling VAT/GST, and reduce your entire legal risk profile.
- Visual: Imagine selling a subscription in Ghana. With Spotflow, we collect the payment via Mobile Money, handle the local tax, and send you the net revenue. No local entity required. Just growth.
