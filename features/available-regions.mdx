## Overview

Spotflow provides a unified payment infrastructure that helps global and African businesses manage collections, disbursements, and multi-currency settlements through a single integration. We offer payment orchestration (**cards**, **mobile money**, **bank transfers**, **EFT**), built-in liquidity and reconciliation, and a compliance rail that handles KYC, AML, and regulatory requirements across markets. 

Our platform is built for enterprises, African businesses, and fintechs/PSPs that want to expand into Africa and other regions without stitching together multiple local providers, supported by developer-first APIs and deep integrations with major PSPs.
