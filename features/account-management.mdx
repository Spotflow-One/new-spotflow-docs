## My Profile: Personalize your space

Merchants can easily access and manage their personal account information for a more customized Spotflow experience. This includes updating contact details and your brand’s logo.

### Access and Update Your Profile
1. Navigate to Profile: Go to Settings → Profile.
2. Edit Details: Here, you can update your Contact Name and upload your Profile Image/Logo for personalization.
3. Save: Click Save to apply the changes.
