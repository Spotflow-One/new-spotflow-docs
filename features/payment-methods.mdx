#### Automatic Activation
No manual setup is required. All supported methods below are pre-configured and managed by Spotflow once your merchant account is verified, region coverages are added and Live Mode is enabled.

#### Supported Payment Methods
Spotflow provides high-conversion, locally-relevant methods across our currently supported 13 African countries.

<Note>
"13 African Countries" refers to the specific nations where Spotflow has integrated the local Bank Transfer, card, EFT, and Mobile Money rails.
</Note>

#### Expanding Payment Options
We are continuously expanding our payment network. If you require a specific payment method not mentioned here, please contact us at [support@spotflow.one](mailto:support@spotflow.one), and we'll certainly consider it for our roadmap.
